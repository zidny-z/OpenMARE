# Hospital Management System: Stakeholder Goals and Needs

## Primary Goals
- Improve patient care coordination by having all relevant information accessible to authorized staff
- Reduce administrative workload for hospital staff through streamlined processes
- Minimize errors in patient records and medication management
- Optimize resource utilization (staff, rooms, equipment, inventory)

## Key Needs
- **Patient Records Management**: Secure, organized access to patient medical histories, treatments, medications, and test results
- **Appointment Scheduling**: Efficient system to book, reschedule, and track patient appointments across departments
- **Inventory Tracking**: Real-time visibility into medical supplies, medications, and equipment availability
- **Staff Coordination**: Clear communication channels between different departments and shifts

## Pain Points to Address
- Time-consuming manual record-keeping and data entry
- Appointment scheduling conflicts and double-booking
- Difficulty tracking inventory levels leading to shortages or overstocking
- Inefficient communication between departments causing delays
- Challenges maintaining accurate, up-to-date patient information
- Compliance with healthcare regulations and data privacy requirements

## Constraints
- Must comply with healthcare regulations (HIPAA/other regional equivalents)
- Need for strict access controls and audit trails
- Integration requirements with existing hospital systems
- Budget limitations typical for healthcare institutions
- Training time for staff with varying technical proficiency
- System reliability requirements (minimal downtime)
- Scalability to handle varying patient volumes

## Success Indicators
- Reduced administrative time spent on record-keeping
- Fewer scheduling conflicts and appointment no-shows
- Improved inventory management with fewer shortages
- Enhanced patient satisfaction through smoother experiences
- Better compliance with healthcare regulations
- Increased staff satisfaction with reduced administrative burden
